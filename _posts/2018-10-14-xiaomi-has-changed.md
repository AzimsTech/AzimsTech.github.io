Xiaomi is so different now. Their used to have great developer support for custom rom. 

## My Problem with Xiaomi Phones

* MIUI has a lot of bugs
* If you upgraded to latest version of MIUI, you will restricted to flash older version unless your bootloader unlocked
* MIUI came with ads built-in into their system apps, you can still disable it though
* Need to wait 720 hours (30 days) tobe able unlock bootloader
* Still has an awful support forum, not big deal

## What I still Hope they continued doing..

* Monthly android security update 
* Affordable redmi devices

## Unlocking bootloader & camera2 API support becoming a feature now

* Xiaomi suddenly allowed Pocophone F1 to be unlocked within 72hours instead 
* Camera2 API can't be enabled on MIUI10 most of their phones. Pocophone, MI8 & and MI MIX 2s as exception.

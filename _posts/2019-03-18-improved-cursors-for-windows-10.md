## Introducing Posy's cursor

![Posy's Cursor comparison](https://i.imgur.com/0zLvh6a.png)

I think this custom cursor is far better than the cursor came with Windows 10 out of the box. Here a few points why I think it is:
1. Thicker and slightly bigger text selection cursor which improved the visibility by a lot.
2. Better looking cursor arrow in my opinion. Though this is subjective to your liking. 
3. Less boring loading animation. Instead of rotation circle, this cursor has old school Hourglass shape which has been forgotten.
4. Better support high for dpi display. The original windows 10 cursor looks just plain simple geometry because of lack of details for higher resolution. So, this cursor already fixed that problem.
